Welcome to the Urbanization & Housing Market Trends website. Explore our interactive visualization to learn more about the trends impacting urban development and housing markets.

About

Background:

  • Urbanization and housing market trends have a profound impact on the economic, social, and environmental landscapes of regions.
  • Understanding these trends is essential for urban planners, policymakers, real estate developers, economists, and the general public. Trends include:
    • Historical Housing Prices
    • Urban vs. Suburban Trends
    • Impact of Urban Development
    • Affordability and Income Inequality

Intended Audience:

  • Urban Planners: To help design and implement effective urban development strategies.
  • Policymakers: To inform the creation of policies that address housing affordability and economic growth.
  • Real Estate Developers: To provide insights into market trends and investment opportunities.
  • Economists: To analyze the economic impacts of housing trends and urbanization.
  • General Public: To increase awareness and understanding of the factors influencing housing markets and urban development, enabling informed decision-making for homebuyers, renters, and community advocates.

Visualization Goals:

  • Employment Trends: To illustrate the changes in employment rates over the years and their correlation with housing market trends.
  • Median House Prices by State: To provide a visual representation of how median house prices vary across different states and how they have evolved over time.
  • Median House Price vs. Median Income: To compare the average median house prices with median incomes, highlighting affordability issues and economic disparities.
  • Historical Trends: To present a comprehensive historical overview of housing market data, allowing for analysis of long-term trends and patterns.
  • Data Interaction: To enable users to interact with the visualizations for a deeper understanding of specific data points and trends.

Data Source: Zillow 1979 - 2018 dataset from Kaggle

  • The Zillow dataset from Kaggle contains comprehensive information on housing prices in the United States from 1979 to 2018. It includes data on various housing metrics, such as median rent prices, home values, and sales volumes, across different regions.
  • Relevant Data Fields:
    • Region Name
    • Region Type
    • Date
    • Median Home Value
    • Median Rent Price
    • Home Sales Volume
    • Price Per Square Foot
